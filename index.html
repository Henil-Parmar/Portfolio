Suipi -


Bringing the beloved Pasefika card game to mobile and the digital world.

The goal of the game is to obtain as many points as possible at the end of a full game. There is a total of each 11 POINTS in each deal. Each player will have a chance to deal. A player may earn points by matching cards and building groups of cards. Regional rules may differ slightly, but standard rules apply. Rules of the game can be found in Settings

3 modes to choose from: Practice / Multiplayer /Play with Friends

In each mode, you will have the choice of playing 1-vs-1 or a team game.

Practice: If you are new to the game or need a refresher on your skills, practice mode familiarizes you to the game without costing you any chips.

Multiplayer: Play 1-on-1 or in a team with other online SUIPI players. A minimum amount of chips are required to play. You choose the entry amount per player and you will be matched with another online player.

Play with Friends: Play 1-on-1 or in a team with friends. Create a table or join a table with a Room Code. A minimum amount of chips are required to play. You set the entry amount and you will get to test your SUIPI skills amongst your friends.

In the game, you can spin the wheel for extra rewards. The leaderboard creates a competitive environment, and different card themes are available for a personalized experience.
